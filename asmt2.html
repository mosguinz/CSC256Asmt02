Convert integer -3 to 8 digit TC Binary. (0.5 point)

First, convert 333 to binary: 3=21+20=3 = 2^1 + 2^0=3=21+20= 0b 0000 0011.

Then negate it:

    0b 0000 0011    (3 in binary)
    0b 1111 1100    (flip bits)
  + 0b 0000 0001    (add one)
  ---------------
    0b 1111 1101    (-3)

−3-3−3 in an 8-bit two’s complement binary is 0b 1111 1101.
